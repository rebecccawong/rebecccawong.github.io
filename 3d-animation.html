Monster (2023)
Final Film
Overview
Time
4 months
Software
Autodesk Maya
Goal
Ideate, develop, model assets for, and animate a short 3D animated film
Purpose
School group project
Monster is a short-animated film that revolves around Amos, a floater who has struggled to accept his different appearance throughout his entire life, but discovers that everyone is different and learns to accept his identity and his horn defect.
Process

Character Design

I sketched out some ideas for a simplistic, cute character with a slightly non-humanoid look so that the rigging and animation would be uncomplicated.
The sketches on the right were done by me.

In the end, we decided to go with a more humanoid looking character to allow for more expressive movements to be shown, even if the rigging will be more complicated.

Sketches done by groupmate

Environment Sketches

Our film has 4 major environments: home, a school classroom, a graduation room, and work. I drew each of these environments and included props that may be added in the background, such as office supplies and bedroom toys.

Graduation

Storyboard

After developing a general story for our film, I was tasked to create the storyboard of the film. I kept in mind camera framing, the space that the characters and objects were taking up, and posing.

All boards were drawn on Clip Studio Paint.

Animation

Using Autodesk Maya, I animated 2 major scenes in Monster: “Walking to Work” and “work” (the entire section). I also animated the camera movements.

Reflection

Along with learning hard skills such as Autodesk Maya, I learned how to work and communicate with a team. Since this project took a full semester to complete, I learned how to boost my teammates strengths and how to find and take advantage of my own. I offered to do work that I was interested in knowledgeable in, such as the animation and storyboard drawings.


If I were to redo this project, I would work on making my animations smoother. Some parts were a bit too choppy and I think that with more practice, they could look more natural and appealing.
