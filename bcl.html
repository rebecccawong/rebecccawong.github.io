About BCLIQUOR

BCLIQUOR has nearly 200 stores in British Columbia, being one of its largest liquor retailers. The Liquor Distribution Board (LDB) has a team of marketing creatives, including a graphic design team who designs for store events and marketing campaigns.

Role

Graphic designer

TASTE Magazine Covers

The seasonally published TASTE magazine by BCLIQUOR is produced in-house. Typically, 3-4 magazines are produced and published each year. Covers for each issue are conceptualized and mocked up for approval several seasons before the issue's publication.

❮ ❯
Role

Cover concept production artist for the TASTE Winter 2025 and Spring 2026 covers.

Brief

The covers were to be reviewed by the Marketing management team. Relative to the previous TASTE covers, these should be push how alcoholic bottles can be showcased in an environment, and creatively represent the taglines, such as “Lighten Up” for Spring.

In-Store Collateral

In-store collateral is fully produced in-house by the design team. New collateral is designed every month and reflects current BCLIQUOR promotions and campaigns. The goal is to be eye-catching (especially in a sea of similarly shaped bottles) and direct consumers to consider how the products may fit into their lives.
