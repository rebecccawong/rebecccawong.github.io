The Athletics Banquet

Douglas College’s Athletics Banquet is an annual event to celebrate the athletes of the Douglas Royals and the end of their sports seasons. The event consisted of a dinner, awards given, and live photography and videography.

My Work

I was in charge of developing the visual style of the event’s content and utilizing it in almost every piece of media showcased in the event. I also filmed and edited video interviews of the athletes during the event.

The Direction

The same background is used for all deliverables, creating cohesion and recognition between them. It has different textures and shapes created from the Douglas Royals branding colours: green, white, and grey.




My Roles

Art director, graphic designer, video editor, videographer.
