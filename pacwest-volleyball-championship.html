The PACWEST Volleyball Championships

The PACWEST Volleyball Championships is an volleyball event involving multiple post-secondary institutions in the Pacific Western Athletic Association. In February 2024, Douglas College hosted the championships, automatically entering their women's and men's volleyball teams into the event.

My Work

I designed and developed the visual identity for the event's print and digital materials, including posters, score templates, and ID badges.

The Direction

The designs, just like the event, are big and bold. Grey and white backgrounds give flexibility for text and images on top, and the spray textured background gives a unique, handmade touch to the posters and social content.

Art director, graphic designer.
