The PACWEST Volleyball Championships

Hosted by Douglas College in 2024, the PACWEST Volleyball Championships is an annual volleyball championship involving post-secondary institutions in the Pacific Western Athletic Association (PACWEST). As the host school, Douglas College’s women’s and men’s volleyball teams were automatically entered into the championships.

Contribution

Print, digital, and web collateral were required for the before, during, and after of the event. This includes marketing artwork for social media, printed wayfinding signage, posters, ID badges, and event guides, and social media posts for athlete shoutouts.

Art director, graphic designer
